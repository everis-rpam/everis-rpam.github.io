Information Requierement and Constraint
Details:
Information Requirement and Constraint.<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/><br/>A Criterion with two Properties:<br/><br/><ol>
<li>An Information Requirement;</li><li>A constrain applied to this Information Requirement.</li></ol>
<br/>This can be used for requirements that are very specific to the SP service. For example, the EFSA (European Food Safety Authority), requires that the eMandate Response specify in the response whether the mandatee(s) has ever breached one specific regulation related to food safety (self-declaration of responsibility).<br/><br/>Information Requirement (IR): The mandatee is not in breach of any of the articles of the regulation...". <br/><br/>Constraint: "Regulation 1924/2006 on nutrition and health claims made on foods" (http://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/ALL/?uri=CELEX:02006R1924-20121129). <br/>
