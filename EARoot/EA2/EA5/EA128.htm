Person who needs another person to act on his/her/its behalf. <br/><br/>Additional Information: <br/><br/>1. The term "mandator" is thus an "umbrella-word" covering the sense of "person that is being surrogated (e.g. replaced, represented, etc.) in a given situation". <br/>2. Persons may be Natural Persons or Legal Persons;<br/>3. The mandator is not necessarily the person that empowers the mandatee;<br/>4. The mandator is not necessarily the creator of the mandate.<br/><br/>
